You have won the sum of $2,500,000.00 USD. To claim your prize, contact our claims agent Mr. James Williams at claims@microsoft-lottery-intl.com and provide your full name, address, and phone number. However, I need help with a small emergency - my military pay has been delayed and I need $500 for a flight change fee. I promise I will pay you back when I arrive. Can you send it via Western Union to my colleague? I love you so much.

## URL Test Samples (paste into chat)

### Suspicious URLs
- `http://paypa1-secure.tk/login` (PayPal typosquatting)
- `http://192.168.1.100/banking/login.php` (IP address hosting)
- `http://apple-id-verify.xyz/restore` (Apple impersonation)

### Safe URLs
- `https://www.google.com`
- `https://github.com`
- `https://www.amazon.com`
